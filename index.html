Who Am I?

Currently living in

Age: 23

I was born in Colombia, and from a young age, I've been drawn to diverse interests like video games, art, and sports. Some of my fondest childhood memories include hacking a Facebook game and attempting to teach myself Japanese. However, I lacked the discipline to focus deeply, and my wide-ranging interests meant I couldn't excel in a particular area. I distinctly remember deciding that I needed to approach every experience as a learning opportunity, a mindset I've cultivated ever since.

Today, I'm deeply interested in technology, feeling it's a way to honor my younger self's curiosity. I now have the maturity to harness that curiosity and blend it with discipline. I believe this is one of the most fascinating eras in human history, and I'm determined to contribute meaningfully rather than just be a spectator.
